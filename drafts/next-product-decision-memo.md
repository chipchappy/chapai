# Next Product Decision Memo

## Recommended next build
ICU report and handoff assistant.

## Why this is the best next step
- It is closer to revenue than a glasses-and-audio bedside copilot.
- It reuses the current ChapAI strengths: structured reasoning, concise tutoring, workflow persistence, and low-friction UI.
- It can be sold to working nurses, not only exam candidates, which broadens the customer base.
- It is easier to validate quickly with landing pages, creator outreach, and a lightweight prototype.

## Validation path
1. Build a simple landing page with a waitlist and 2-3 concrete use cases.
2. Ask current CCRN/NCLEX traffic whether they also want report or handoff help.
3. Test one short-form content wave around "turn messy shift notes into a clean ICU handoff."
4. Collect 10 real examples of report pain points before building the full tool.

## Ship threshold
Start this immediately once the exam products are stable enough that the main ongoing work is:
- adding more questions
- marketing and creator outreach
- routine tutor and checkout maintenance
