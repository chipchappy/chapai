# Affiliate and Collab Offer Menu

Use the lightest offer first. Only expand if they actually care.

## Offer 1: Free founder pass
- Best for:
  - first contact
  - small creators
  - educators who want to try it before posting
- What they get:
  - private access to the package
  - time to test it
  - permission to give honest feedback

## Offer 2: Creator code
- Best for:
  - creators who have an engaged audience
  - pages that already post study or nursing content
- What they get:
  - a simple discount code
  - tracked clicks
  - a clean reason to share the product

## Offer 3: Story or reel mention
- Best for:
  - creators who are open but not ready for a formal partnership
- What they get:
  - a simple one-time paid or unpaid mention
  - a clean caption and asset
  - no heavy obligation

## Offer 4: Affiliate rev share
- Best for:
  - creators who already trust their audience
  - pages with recurring engagement
- What they get:
  - commission on clicks or sales
  - a clear link
  - a low-friction path to support the product

## Offer 5: Bundle or cohort access
- Best for:
  - nurse coaches
  - bootcamp hosts
  - educators who run groups
- What they get:
  - group access
  - package discount
  - content they can use with their own students

## Suggested order
- Start with founder pass.
- Move to creator code.
- Use affiliate only when there is real interest.
- Keep the ask small until they prove fit.
