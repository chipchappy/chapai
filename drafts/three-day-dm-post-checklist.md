# 3-Day DM + Post Execution Checklist

## Day 1
- Pick 10 creators, 5 CCRN and 5 NCLEX.
- Send 5 warm DMs.
- Post 1 X post with the strongest package angle.
- Post 1 Instagram story or reel hook.
- Log every response in one note.

### Day 1 message goal
- Keep it founder-led.
- Ask for a private preview, not a big commitment.
- Mention the right package and the reason it exists.

## Day 2
- Send the other 5 DMs.
- Follow up with anyone who viewed but did not reply.
- Post 1 CCRN-specific X post.
- Post 1 NCLEX-specific X post.
- Share 1 package card or clean screenshot.

### Day 2 message goal
- Make the product feel useful, not promotional.
- Keep the tone calm and specific.

## Day 3
- Send 3 short follow-ups.
- Post 1 founder-style “why we built this” message.
- Post 1 direct package CTA.
- Identify the best 2 creators for a second pass.
- Decide who gets a founder code, affiliate offer, or preview access.
