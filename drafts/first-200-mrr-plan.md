# First $200 MRR Plan

## Goal
- Reach the first 4 to 7 paid customers quickly enough to clear roughly `$200/month` in recurring revenue.

## Fastest path
- Sell the separate packages, not a generic blended product:
  - `CCRN package`
  - `NCLEX package`
- Push traffic to the live upgrade page:
  - [https://clarityccrn.chapaisolutions.com/upgrade](https://clarityccrn.chapaisolutions.com/upgrade)

## Why this is realistic
- The product is already live.
- Stripe checkout is working.
- Separate package framing matches two different buyer intents.
- Nemoclaw can keep adding fresh questions without heavy token burn.

## 7-day sprint
1. Post one sharp product clip or screenshot every day on Instagram and X.
2. Alternate the offer:
   - day A = CCRN
   - day B = NCLEX
3. Link every social surface to the upgrade page, not the homepage.
4. Lead with:
   - original questions
   - clean visual rationales
   - AI-guided next-step study flow
5. Add one urgent CTA:
   - `Founding access`
   - `Early pricing`
   - `First 25 nurses`

## Fast experiments
- Experiment 1:
  - headline test on `/upgrade`
  - `Choose your exam. Get a cleaner question system.`
  - vs
  - `Original CCRN and NCLEX prep built for faster decisions.`
- Experiment 2:
  - one screenshot carousel from the dashboard/brains story for behind-the-scenes trust
- Experiment 3:
  - one direct founder post:
    - `I built separate CCRN and NCLEX study packages because the test-takers are different and deserved different prep flows.`

## Offer target
- CCRN:
  - position around ICU nurse confidence, hemodynamics, visual rationale, cleaner study system
- NCLEX:
  - position around prioritization, safety, pharmacology, repeat test-takers, cleaner review path

## Immediate KPI targets
- 50 targeted visits to `/upgrade`
- 5 checkout starts
- 2 conversions
- 1 testimonial or DM screenshot

## What to improve next if traffic lands but conversion lags
- logo and social avatar polish
- stronger package-specific trust copy
- clearer proof block above pricing
- one stronger visual rationale demo on each exam landing
