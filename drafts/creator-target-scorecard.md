# Creator Target Scorecard

Use this to score potential nursing creators before you DM them. Keep the list small and warm. Aim for people who already teach, review, or talk to your exact buyer.

## Scoring
- `5` = excellent fit
- `3` = maybe, worth a test
- `1` = weak fit, only if you have spare time
