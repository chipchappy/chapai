# Next Product Scout Shortlist

## 1. Nurse shift co-pilot
- Voice-first bedside assistant for prioritization, reminders, handoff prep, and escalation checklists.
- Strongest long-term upside, but technically heavier and should wait until exam prep is cash-flowing.

## 2. ICU report + handoff assistant
- Helps nurses turn messy notes into cleaner handoff structures.
- More realistic near-term than full glasses/audio assistance.

## 3. AI clinical reasoning simulator
- Case-based decision trainer for nurses already in practice.
- Natural extension from the current question and tutor engine.

## 4. Nurse educator creator toolkit
- Helps nurse creators turn teaching points into carousels, posts, short scripts, and mini lesson packs.
- Very aligned with the current growth/distribution push.

## 5. Shift reflection + growth tracker
- Helps nurses capture what happened on shift, what they learned, and what to review next.
- Could share brain/persistence concepts with the current agent system.
