# Educator And Health-System Target List

## Priority systems with active nursing education / residency signals
- [HCA Healthcare Nursing Careers](https://www.hcahealthcare.com/nurses/nursing-careers)
  - Angle: large new-grad and specialty-transition footprint, critical-care upskilling, certification support.
- [Providence Professional Development](https://providence.jobs/development/)
  - Angle: Clinical Academy, new and new-to-specialty nurse development, practical education fit.
- [Mayo Clinic Nurse Residency and Training Programs](https://jobs.mayoclinic.org/nurseresidencyprograms)
  - Angle: formal residency structure and high-trust premium positioning.
- [Johns Hopkins Institute for Nursing](https://www.hopkinsmedicine.org/institute-nursing)
  - Angle: continuing education, leadership development, visitor programs, educator credibility.
- [Johns Hopkins Howard County RN Residency](https://www.hopkinsmedicine.org/johns-hopkins-howard-county/nursing/nurse-residency)
  - Angle: transition-to-practice decision makers and nurse residency leads.
- [Cleveland Clinic Nursing Career Exploration](https://my.clevelandclinic.org/departments/nursing/learn-here/career-exploration)
  - Angle: learner growth, career exploration, continuing pipeline support.
- [Cleveland Clinic Professional Growth and Development](https://my.clevelandclinic.org/departments/nursing/career)
  - Angle: nurse education and professional development leaders.
- [Cleveland Clinic Center for Health Professions Education](https://my.clevelandclinic.org/departments/health-sciences-education)
  - Angle: educator and learner support across health professions with room for pilot language.

## Educator-facing targets
- Hospital nurse educator
- ICU clinical educator
- Nurse residency program coordinator
- Professional development specialist
- Transition-to-practice program lead
- Nursing continuing education lead

## Health-system-facing targets
- Nursing education department
- Staff development office
- ICU service line leadership
- Critical care residency / fellowship education office
- Health-system workforce development team
- Learning and development director for nursing services

## Best first contact angle
- Start with education teams already responsible for onboarding, transition-to-practice, or certification support.
- Offer a small pilot rather than a broad enterprise pitch.
- Lead with cleaner prep, lighter learner friction, daily question emails, and easier implementation.
- Show the $10 sprint as a low-friction entry point for last-minute learners.

## Best first ask
- 10-20 learner pilot
- one unit or one cohort
- fixed short founder-rate
- feedback loop after 2 weeks
- optional daily-question email pilot for free lead capture
